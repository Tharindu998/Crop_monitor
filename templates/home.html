Growing Degree Days & Growth Stage Prediction

Growing Degree Days (GDD) is a measure of heat accumulation used to predict plant growth stages. By tracking temperature over time, farmers can estimate when crops will reach key growth milestones. This helps in planning irrigation, fertilization, and harvesting more efficiently.
